Possible causes of symptoms.
Each infectious disease has its own specific signs and symptoms. General signs and symptoms common to a number of infectious diseases include: Fever, Diarrhea, Fatigue, Muscle aches or Coughing etc.
When to see a Doctor.
Seek medical attention if you: Have been bitten by an animal, Are having trouble breathing, Have been coughing for more than a week, Have severe headache with fever, Experience a rash or swelling, Have unexplained or prolonged fever, Have sudden vision problems etc.
